What is Backstage View?

The Backstage view has been introduced in Word 2010 and acts as the central place for managing your documents. The backstage view helps in creating new documents, saving and opening documents, printing and sharing documents, and so on. Getting to the Backstage View is easy: Just click the File tab, located in the upper-left corner of the Word Ribbon.
